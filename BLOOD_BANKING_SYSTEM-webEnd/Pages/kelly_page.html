OVERVIEW

This system is designed to manage, collect,test and distribute blood and blood products. It ensures the safety and availability of blood for transfusions and other medical needs

Frequently asked questions?

What is the process of blood donating?

This process includes registration, a health history review and mini-physical, and post-donation refreshments and recovery.

How often can I donate blood?

Whole blood can be donated every 8 weeks,platelets every 7 days up to 24 times a year, and a plasma every 28 days.

What are the requirements needed for donating blood?

Donors must be in good health, at least 17 years of age under parental guidance or their parent's consent.You should weigh at least 50kg.
