Welcome to the Blood Banking System

Why Request Blood?
 we understand that timely access to blood can be a matter of life and death. If you or a loved one is in need of a blood transfusion, please don't hesitate to request blood from our hospital.

Request Blood When Needed: If you or someone you know requires a transfusion, reach out to us immediately. We are here to support you with the highest standards of medical care.

Why Donate Blood?
 we believe in the power of compassion and the extraordinary impact of a single act of kindness. We invite you to join us in our life-saving mission by donating blood. Your donation can make a profound difference in the lives of patients in need.

Donate Blood: Your donation is a simple act with profound consequences. It takes just a little time, but the impact lasts a lifetime. Our skilled and compassionate team will ensure your donation experience is safe and comfortable.
