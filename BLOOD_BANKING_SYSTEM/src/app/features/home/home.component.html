2

3

4

You'll Feel Great

There's no better feeling than knowing you've made a difference.

What Happens to Your Donation?

Your blood donation goes through careful testing and processing before being distributed to local hospitals and medical facilities. One donation can save up to three lives!

How to Donate

1

Check Eligibility: Most healthy adults can donate. Check our eligibility criteria to see if you qualify.

2

Schedule an Appointment: Use our online booking system or call us to set up a convenient time.

3

Prepare: Eat well and stay hydrated before your appointment.

4

Donate: Our friendly staff will guide you through the process and answer any questions you have.
